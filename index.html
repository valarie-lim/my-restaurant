Malaysia Authentic Cuisine

Welcome to MY Réstaurant, where every dish tells a story of tradition, passion, and homegrown flavours. Founded in 2002, our restaurant celebrates Malaysia’s vibrant food culture – from the bustling streets of Kuala Lumpur to the peaceful kampung kitchens across the nation.
